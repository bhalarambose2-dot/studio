
# HALORA - Sahi Safar, Sahi Nivesh

Welcome to the official production-ready version of **HALORA**, India's premier heritage travel network.

## 🚀 Deployment & Domain Setup

This app is optimized for **Firebase Spark Tier (Free)**. No credit card or billing account is required for standard operations.

### How to add your Custom Domain (e.g., halora.in):
1. Go to the [Firebase Console](https://console.firebase.google.com/).
2. Select your project: `br-courierside`.
3. Navigate to **Hosting** in the left sidebar.
4. Click on **"Add Custom Domain"**.
5. Enter your domain name (e.g., `www.halora.com`).
6. Follow the instructions to update your DNS records (A records or CNAME) on your domain provider (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.).
7. Once verified, Firebase will automatically provision an **SSL Certificate** for free.

## ✨ Features
- **Cinematic Discovery**: Video-based destination guides for Rajasthan.
- **Animated Atmosphere**: Twinkling night sky background with smooth performance.
- **Sahi Safar Booking**: 2 Days / 2 Nights heritage packages at best per-person rates.
- **Smart Wallet**: UPI-integrated wallet for "Pay After Safar" transactions.
